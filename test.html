ECE 650: Systems Programming & Engineering

Section 01, Spring 2018

Teer 203 - Tues/Thurs 8:30-9:45am

This course focuses on a range of topics that are central to both the design of operating systems and the programming system-level software. Students will apply knowledge of basic concepts in operating systems, networking, and programming towards these two areas.
